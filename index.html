Who We Are

Naivasha Young Investors is a youth-led agribusiness organization based in Naivasha, committed to growing organic vegetables, empowering youth, and strengthening food security through sustainable agriculture.

What We Do

Organic Vegetable Farming

We grow high-quality organic vegetables using environmentally friendly and climate-smart practices.

Youth Agripreneurship

We engage and empower young people through hands-on farming, training, and mentorship.

Food Security

Our work contributes to reliable food supply and improved nutrition within local communities.
